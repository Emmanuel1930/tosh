Working Mode of Operation for the constituted Legal Arm/Department

  • By Empyrean Humanitarian Foundation
  • AUG 23, 2023
  • 12 Comments

As an organization that strongly frowns upon injustice and the severe oppression of the poor by the wealthy class, one of our primary objectives and mandates is to defend and represent the underprivileged with in our society. To this end, we have decided to establish a dedicated Legal Department. This department, will comprised of brilliant and experienced lawyers, and it is tasked with the critical responsibility of representing the oppressed, upholding their fundamental human rights as enshrined in the constitution, and ensuring that justice is served by prosecuting oppressors, fraudsters, those who extort by false pretense or does take undue advantages to scam others unjustly.

Mode of Operation

The Legal Department will actively identify cases of injustice, oppression, and fraud within the community, focusing on those affecting the underprivileged. Cases may be brought to the departmentâ€™s attention through direct complaints, community outreach, or referrals from other departments and organizations. Each case will undergo a thorough assessment to determine the legal merits and the potential impact on the individual and the broader community.

The department will provide pro bono legal representation to individuals who are unable to afford the high costs of legal services. This representation will cover a wide range of issues, including but not limited to human rights violations, property disputes, unlawful detentions, extortion and cases of fraud. Lawyers within the department will work tirelessly to ensure that the rights of the oppressed are protected and that they receive fair treatment in all legal proceedings.

OUR ADVOCACY PROGRAM: EMPOWERING YOUTH THROUGH AWARENESS AND PUBLIC SENSITIZATION

The Empyrean Humanitarian Foundation (EHF) recognizes that the youth are not just the leaders of tomorrow but the agents of change today. In a society where political manipulation and economic disenfranchisement are pervasive, EHF is dedicated to liberating young people from the shackles of political enslavement and financial disempowerment. Our advocacy program is designed to educate, inspire, and mobilize youth to understand their rights, recognize their potential, and actively participate in creating a more just and equitable society.

In many regions, youth are often exploited by political systems that prioritize power over progress. These systems may use youths as tools in conflicts, suppress their voices, and limit their opportunities to engage in meaningful decision-making processes. EHF's advocacy program is committed to enlightening young people about the mechanisms of political manipulation and the importance of political literacy

Through our advocacy program, Empyrean Humanitarian Foundation aims to create a generation of young leaders who are politically savvy, financially empowered, and committed to social justice. By breaking the chains of political enslavement and financial dependence, we believe that the youth can drive the social and economic transformation needed to build a more equitable society. Our future goals include expanding our programs to reach more young people across the country, developing more comprehensive training modules, and establishing a network of youth advocates who can continue this important work in their communities. Empyrean Humanitarian Foundation's advocacy program is more than just an educational initiative; it is a movement towards the emancipation of the youth from the constraints of political and economic oppression. By empowering young people with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed, we are investing in a brighter, more equitable future for all.
